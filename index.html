About the Event

Sparking Innovation

The SMVITM Ideathon is a one-day event aimed at fostering innovation and creativity among students. Organized by the CU Club at SMVITM, this event encourages teams of four to present their unique projects in any format, such as a PowerPoint or prototype.


Each team will have 10 minutes to showcase their ideas, followed by a 5-minute Q&A session with judges and other participants, during which they may potentially lose points. Points are awarded based on the uniqueness of the project, its real-world application, and the quality of the presentation and Q&A.


Our goal is to create a collaborative environment where brilliant minds can exchange ideas and inspire each other. This event is a great opportunity for students to demonstrate their innovative thinking and problem-solving skills, with exciting cash prizes.

Event Schedule

Stay informed and make the most of the Ideathon with our detailed schedule

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Registration Verification and Refreshments

Participants arrive and register for the event. Refreshments are available for attendees.

10:00 AM - 10:30 AM

Introduction and Welcome Speech

Welcome speech by the event organizer to introduce the agenda and objectives of the day. Participants are encouraged to gather and prepare for the first session.

10:30 AM - 11:30 AM

Start Program - First Round of Presentations

The event kicks off with the first round of presentations, covering topics related to innovation and technology trends. Each presenter has 15 minutes to showcase their research and findings.

11:30 AM - 11:45 AM

Tea Break

Short break for participants to refresh and network.

11:45 AM - 1:00 PM

Start Program - Second Round of Presentations

Continuation of presentations with a focus on case studies and real-world applications in the industry.

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

Lunch Break

Participants break for lunch and informal discussions.

2:00 PM - 3:30 PM

Start Program - Third Round of Presentations

Presentations continue with in-depth explorations of emerging technologies and their impact on the industry.

3:30 PM - 3:45 PM

Break

Short break to recharge and prepare for the final sessions.

3:45 PM - 5:00 PM

Start Program - Final Round of Presentations

Last set of presentations highlighting key takeaways and future directions in the field.

5:00 PM - 5:30 PM

End Speech and Announcements

Concluding remarks and announcements by the event organizer.

5:30 PM - 6:00 PM

Certificates Distribution and Exit

Distribution of certificates to participants and closing of the event.
