Registration Guidelines

  • Both the software and hardware solutions are accepted at Hackothsava - 2k24.
  • Registration to Hackothsava consists of several steps; your team is not considered registered until you complete all the steps and see “Submit” on the register page.
  • The steps require you to fill in all the details, including the team formation part where a leader must create a team and share the team name with teammates to join.
  • A verification mail will be sent to the registered email of the team leader and team members for confirmation, which must be confirmed.
  • Only the team leader can proceed to the step of idea submission.
  • A team can consist of a minimum of 2 members and a maximum of 4 members belonging to the same college.

Final Step: Idea Submission Details

Guidelines for Idea Submission

  • Problem Statement
  • Solution
  • Business Idea
  • Future Enhancement
  • Keep it concise to 5 pages. Keep text concise and avoid paragraphs. Use bullet points for clarity. Include images if necessary for better understanding (optional).
  • Team Information: Ensure to fill in your details on the first page, including your college name and state.
  • Submission: Confirm the file size does not exceed 5MB and is in the form of ppt, pdf, or pptx. Rename the file to Team_Name.pdf before submitting it.
  • There will be a shortlisting of teams based on various criteria like how creative the features sound, how innovative the idea is, whether it is market fit, feasibility, relevance to the chosen track, etc.
  • The top teams will be announced in the 4th week of July 2024, who will be participating in Hackothsava - 2k24.
